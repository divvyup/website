pay for what you eat. not what you don't.

You don't eat the same things as your roommates. Trying to figure out who owes what on a reciept is a pain, and making an extra trip to the store just for yourself is even more inconvenient.
That's where we come in.
Sign up, make a group, then buy some food and give us the receipt or eReceipt. We'll parse everything out, all you have to do is tap on the things you eat. We'll automatically split the cost between you and your roommates and we'll even remember what you eat for next time.
